weight cut meal

For grapplers (jiu jitsu and wrestling), here is a great weight cut meal that does not retain water.

Ingredients

  • ground turkey
  • seasoning: paprika, onion, garlic, salt, black pepper
  • sweet potatoes
  • any oil

Steps

  1. wash sweet potato and cut them into appropriate wedges
  2. add olive oil and paprika (if you like a kick to them)
  3. put them in the airfryer for 20 mins at 380
  4. while waiting, heat a pan with tbsp of oil
  5. mix ground chicken in a seperate bowl with all seasonings
  6. once heated, turn to medium heat and add ground chicken mix
  7. stir for 2 minutes and let sit for 2 minutes, then stir again
  8. repeat the process until thoroughly cooked
  9. once sweet potatoes are cooked, add them to a plate with the ground chicken
    10. bon appetite!
